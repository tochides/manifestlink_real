How to Use ManifestLink

Complete guide for passengers and port staff

Follow these steps to register and use your QR code

1

Register Online

Fill out the pre-registration form with your personal details, travel information, and ID details.

2

Receive QR Code

3

Tips & Best Practices

For Passengers

  • Register at least 24 hours before travel
  • Keep your QR code easily accessible
  • Have your government ID ready
  • Arrive at port 30 minutes before departure

For Staff

  • Ensure good lighting for QR scanning
  • Verify passenger ID matches QR data
  • Handle manual entries carefully
  • Report technical issues immediately

Security & Privacy

  • All data is encrypted and secure
  • Compliant with RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act)
  • Access logs are maintained
  • Regular security audits conducted

About ManifestLink

ManifestLink is an innovative automated passenger manifest system designed specifically for Guimaras Port operations. Our solution addresses the challenges of traditional paper-based manifest systems by leveraging QR technology and real-time data management.

Project Rationale

Traditional passenger manifest systems are time-consuming, error-prone, and lack real-time visibility. ManifestLink eliminates these inefficiencies while ensuring compliance with maritime regulations and data privacy laws.

Technical Specifications

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript with React.js
  • Backend: Node.js with Express.js
  • Database: MongoDB for real-time data management
  • QR Integration: ZXing library for scanning/generation
  • Security: HTTPS, JWT authentication, data encryption
