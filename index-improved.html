How It Works

Passenger Registration

Passengers register online with their personal information and receive a unique QR code.

2

QR Code Scanning

At the port, staff scan the passenger's QR code to verify identity and update manifest.

3

Boarding Confirmation

Passenger status is updated in real-time, confirming boarding and departure tracking.
